Scott Melnick

Security Speaker & Gaming Technology Expert

Speaker Bio

Scott Melnick is a seasoned speaker and expert in gaming technology and cybersecurity with over 25+ years. With a wealth of knowledge spanning casino technology, cloud systems, and AI convergence in gaming, he’s recognized for his unique insights and hands-on experience. His talks and publications are highly regarded across the gaming and security industries. Scott is currently over the gaming security department for AGS a supplier of B2B RMG and Social gaming as well as land based Slots. Previously he spent 6 years at Bulletproof, A GLI company doing Pentesting for the casino industry and heading up research and development and investigations into cheating and the gray market.
